WHAT IS PS?

Public Speaking is the skill of structuring and delivering a clear, engaging, and impactful oral presentation to an audience, focusing on confidence, body language, and effective communication.

Core Rules of PS

The Rules of Public Speaking:

  • 1. The 30-Second Hook

    Capture attention instantly at the start with a question, story, or shocking fact.

  • 2. The 10-20-30 Slide Rule

    Maximum 10 slides, maximum 20 minutes, and minimum 30pt font size.

  • 3. Audience-Centric Tone

    Adapt your language and topic to match the listeners' background.

  • 4. 3-Second Eye Contact

    Look at individual audience members for 3 seconds to build a real connection.

  • 5. Vocal & Physical Variety

    Use deliberate hand gestures, open posture, and change your tone/pitch to avoid sounding robotic.

  • 6. Direct Q&A Method

    State your answer directly first, then back it up with a brief explanation.
