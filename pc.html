Professional Communication

  • 🎮 Main Game

Definition

Professional Communication is the practice of exchanging information efficiently and politely within a business or corporate environment. Unlike casual talking, PC focuses on clear, respectful, and goal-oriented communication to build trust with colleagues, managers, and clients.

Core Rules

  • 1. Be Clear and Concise

    Get straight to the point. Avoid long, confusing explanations so that the readers can understand your message instantly without wasting time.

  • 2. Maintain Professional Politeness

    Always use respectful greetings and a positive tone, even when you are delivering bad news or handling a complaint from a client.

  • 3. Know Your Audience

    Adjust your vocabulary depending on who you are talking to. Speaking to your company's CEO or a formal client requires a different approach than chatting with a close teammate.
